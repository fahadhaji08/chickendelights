  HOW TO ADD A PHOTO FOR A SPECIFIC PRODUCT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Drop your photo into THIS folder: public/products/items/

The file name must match the product name, converted to
lowercase with spaces replaced by hyphens.

EXAMPLES:
  Product Name               →  File to drop here
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────
  Chicken Samosa             →  chicken-samosa.jpg
  Chicken Tandoori Samosa    →  chicken-tandoori-samosa.jpg
  Chicken Malai Samosa       →  chicken-malai-samosa.jpg
  Chicken Seekh Kabab        →  chicken-seekh-kabab.jpg
  Chicken Cheese Balls       →  chicken-cheese-balls.jpg
  Fried Chicken Boneless     →  fried-chicken-boneless.jpg
  Crispy Chicken Tender      →  crispy-chicken-tender.jpg

RULE: lowercase + hyphens + .jpg (or .jpeg, .png, .webp)
      No spaces, no capitals, no special characters.

HOW IT WORKS (3-level fallback):
  1. The site tries the per-item photo here first.
  2. If not found, it falls back to the section photo
     (e.g. public/products/samosa.jpg).
  3. If that's also missing, it shows "Photo Coming Soon".

So you can add photos one at a time — the rest continue
to show the section photo until you're ready.
